## Description

**Jump Droid** is a vertical rocket exploration game where you pilot a droid-controlled spacecraft through 12 hostile atmospheric zones. Manage fuel, heat, shields, and hull integrity while dodging environmental hazards, destroying enemy drones, and defeating colossal bosses. Customize your build with 4 rocket classes and 17 modules. Discover hidden signals, unlock artifacts, and ascend beyond 100,000 meters to face The Singularity.

---

## History

Jump Droid began as a hands-on project to explore the limits of Jetpack Compose Canvas for game development. What started as a simple vertical scroller grew into a full-featured physics-driven exploration game with 11 EPICs of development spanning atmospheric zones, boss AI, modular rocket builds, a mission system, and a complete endgame with prestige mechanics. The project is now open source under the MIT License.

---

## Key Features

- **12 altitude zones** — Each with unique parallax backgrounds, hazards, enemies, and dynamic BGM
- **11 boss encounters** — Multi-phase fights with weak point targeting, each with distinct AI
- **26+ threat types** — Hazards, enemies, mini-bosses, and screen-filling bosses
- **Modular rocket builds** — 4 classes, 17 unlockable modules, infinite customization
- **12-track mission system** — Evolving tier progression from Rookie to God
- **Prestige system** — Permanent +10% hull/shield per reset beyond 100km
- **Eternal Mode** — Capped infinite scaling past 100,000 meters
- **Hidden content** — Secret missions, lore signals, artifacts with set bonuses
- **Open source** — MIT License, built entirely with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose
