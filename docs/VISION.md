# Jump Droid — Vision & Roadmap

## Core Design Philosophy

**Jump Droid is not an endless score chaser. It is a vertical exploration and discovery game.**

The primary player motivation should be:

- *What is above me?*
- *What have I not discovered?*
- *What secrets remain?*
- *How can I improve my ship?*
- *How far can I climb?*

### What We Reward

| Value | How |
|-------|-----|
| **Exploration** | Reaching new zones, finding artifacts, revealing lore |
| **Curiosity** | Discovery system, codex entries, environmental storytelling |
| **Mastery** | Combo chaining, resource management, boss weak-point destruction |
| **Skill** | Tight touch controls, precise landing, heat/fuel timing |
| **Discovery** | 43 codex entries, artifact tracking, ascension rank progression |

### What We Avoid

- Excessive grinding for progression
- Pay-to-win mechanics
- Progression that replaces skill
- Combat overwhelming exploration
- Turning the game into a pure idle progression system
- Features that do not support the core fantasy of exploration and ascension

## The Five Design Pillars

### 1. Boss Encounters

The game's strongest differentiator. No other vertical climber has boss fights with destructible weak points, phase-based AI, and set-piece moments. The Gatekeeper's rotating orbital ring, Star-Eater's suction pull, Leviathan's segmented body — these transform the climb from a reflex test into a series of escalating confrontations.

### 2. Resource Management Triangle

Fuel, heat, shield, and hull create constant decision-making. Every 2-second thrust is a commitment. Four interacting systems — fuel limits thrust, heat limits duration, shield absorbs damage, hull ends the run — create a richer decision space than any single-resource climber.

### 3. Combo Chaining

The combo system connects platforming skill to survival. Aggressive players who chain different platform types are rewarded with shield capsules and hull repairs. The risk-reward loop — take harder platforms to keep the combo alive, get survival supplies as a reward — is the game's most elegant design.

### 4. Discovery / Codex System

43 entries give the game a metroidvania-style collectible layer. The lore entries tell a genuine sci-fi story (The Great Signal, lost expeditions, artifacts). The artifact system (Flight Recorder, Unknown Alloy, Encrypted Beacon, Drone Core) is a storytelling mechanism, not just a collectible.

### 5. Ascent Journey

Six visually distinct zones are the game's progression structure and identity. Each changes the background, hazards, platform mix, and difficulty — from Earth's blue skies to the Void's reality-distorting black.

## Player Experience Goals

| Stage | Mindset | What Drives Them |
|-------|---------|-----------------|
| **New player** | Curiosity | First zone exploration, learning controls, first discoveries |
| **Experienced player** | Mastery | Combo optimization, boss strategies, rocket unlocks |
| **Veteran player** | Completion | All 43 codex entries, ascension rank, permanent upgrades |

The feeling at every stage should be: **"There is something higher waiting for me."**

## Roadmap

### Current State (Completed Foundations)

- Core flight mechanics and touch controls
- Fuel system with consumption, recharge, and capacity upgrades
- Heat system with overheat lockout and cooling mechanics
- 10 platform types with unique physics and rendering
- 43-entry discovery/codex system with persistence
- In-game archive screen for viewing discoveries
- Ascension Rank (Explorer I–V) with permanent stat upgrades
- 15-mission system across 4 tracks with ceremony animations
- 31 threats across 4 tiers (hazards, enemies, mini-bosses, bosses)
- Shield system with segmented visual and regen delay
- Hull integrity with 2.5-second destruction sequence
- Survival economy (combo → reward loop)

## Success Criteria

A player should eventually feel that they are commanding an evolving spacecraft on a journey through increasingly dangerous and mysterious regions of the sky and beyond.

The game should feel like an expedition — not simply an arcade score chase.
