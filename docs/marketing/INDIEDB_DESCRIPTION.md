## Title

Jump Droid

## Tagline

Ascend Beyond the Atmosphere

## Description

Jump Droid is a precision vertical exploration game built entirely with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose Canvas. You control a droid-piloted rocket ascending through 12 increasingly hostile atmospheric layers to reach The Void and beyond.

### Gameplay

The core loop is simple: ascend, survive, discover, upgrade, ascend further. You must manage fuel, heat, shields, and hull integrity while navigating environmental hazards, fighting enemy drones, and defeating colossal bosses.

### Bosses

Each zone introduces new threats. The 11 boss encounters are the highlight — multi-phase fights with unique AI patterns, weak point targeting, and escalating intensity. The final meta-boss, The Singularity, warps the HUD and challenges everything you've learned.

### Customization

Your rocket is not a vehicle — it's a Build. Choose from 4 rocket classes and customize with 17 unlockable modules. Optimize for survivability, speed, or raw firepower.

### Endgame

Reach 100,000 meters to unlock prestige (permanent +10% hull/shield per reset), Omega Modules (Void Engine, Singularity Core), and Eternal Mode — capped infinite scaling that lets you ascend forever.

### Open Source

Jump Droid is fully open source under the MIT License. The complete codebase is available on GitHub, built entirely with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose Canvas — no third-party game engine.

### Features

- 12 atmospheric zones with unique visual themes and BGM
- 11 multi-phase boss encounters
- 26+ threat types (hazards, enemies, mini-bosses)
- 14 platform types with unique mechanics
- 4 rocket classes, 17 modules
- 12-track mission system
- Power-ups, artifacts, set bonuses
- Secret missions, hidden lore signals, Codex
- Prestige system and Eternal Mode
