# Jump Droid — Brand Guide

**Last Updated:** 2026-07-14

---

## Brand Identity

Jump Droid is a vertical rocket exploration game. The brand voice is precise, atmospheric, and discovery-driven.

### Core Values

- **Physics-first**: Movement and collision are the soul of the experience
- **Hard but fair**: High difficulty balanced by robust recovery mechanics
- **Modular progression**: Your rocket is a Build, not a vehicle
- **Mystery & discovery**: Hidden content rewards deep exploration

---

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo

The primary logo is `media/logos/jump-droid-hero.png`. It should be used on light or dark backgrounds with sufficient contrast.

### Icon

The app icon `media/logos/jump-droid-icon.png` is used for the Android launcher and store listings.

### Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the rocket icon on all sides of the logo.

### Incorrect Usage

- Do not stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
- Do not apply drop shadows or effects
- Do not place on low-contrast backgrounds
- Do not replace or modify the typeface

## Color Palette

The game uses a sci-fi industrial palette defined in code. For marketing materials:

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| SciFiBackground | `#1A1A2E` | Dark backgrounds |
| SciFiSurface | `#16213E` | Card surfaces |
| SciFiAccent | `#0F3460` | Accent elements |
| SciFiHighlight | `#E94560` | Highlights, CTAs |
| SciFiGreen | `#00E676` | Positive states |
| SciFiRed | `#FF5252` | Critical states |
| SciFiCyan | `#00BCD4` | Information |
| SciFiGold | `#FFD700` | Rewards, achievements |

---

## Typography

For marketing and store copy, use clean sans-serif fonts:

- **Headlines**: Montserrat Bold or Inter Bold
- **Body**: Inter Regular or Roboto Regular
- **Code/Monospace**: JetBrains Mono or Fira Code

---

## Tone of Voice

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Store description | Professional, atmospheric, feature-focused |
| Social media | Engaging, community-driven, discovery-oriented |
| Press release | Formal, milestone-focused |
| Bug reports / support | Helpful, transparent, concise |

### Do

- Use atmospheric language: "ascend," "discover," "survive"
- Reference altitude, zones, and progression
- Emphasize the open-source nature

### Don't

- Overhype or make promises about future content
- Use excessive jargon
- Compare directly to competitors
