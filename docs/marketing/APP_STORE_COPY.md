## Long Description

**Jump Droid** is a precision vertical exploration simulator. Pilot a droid-controlled rocket through 12 hostile atmospheric zones, from Earth's surface to the edge of The Singularity.

### GAMEPLAY

Manage fuel, heat, shields, and hull integrity as you ascend through increasingly dangerous layers of atmosphere. Dodge environmental hazards, destroy enemy drones, and defeat colossal screen-filling bosses. Every zone introduces new threats, new platform types, and new visual themes.

### BOSS ENCOUNTERS

Face 11 unique bosses, each with multi-phase attack patterns and weak point targeting. From the Cloud Commander to the reality-warping Singularity, every boss fight demands precision and adaptation.

### BUILD YOUR ROCKET

Choose from 4 rocket classes and customize with 17 unlockable modules. Your rocket is a Build — optimize for survivability, speed, or firepower.

### MISSION SYSTEM

Progress through 12 mission tracks that evolve from Rookie to God tier. Complete challenges, unlock rewards, and discover hidden signals.

### ENDGAME

Beyond 100,000 meters lies The Singularity — the final meta-boss. Defeat it to unlock prestige, Omega Modules, and Eternal Mode with infinite capped scaling.

### FEATURES

- 12 unique atmospheric zones with parallax backgrounds
- 11 boss encounters with distinct AI and phases
- 26+ threat types including hazards, enemies, and mini-bosses
- 14 platform types with unique traversal mechanics
- 4 rocket classes and 17 modules for deep customization
- 12-track mission system with evolving tier progression
- Power-ups, artifacts with set bonuses, and hidden lore
- Prestige system for permanent progression
- Eternal Mode for infinite ascent
- Secret missions, blueprints, and Codex discovery
- Open source — built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose
