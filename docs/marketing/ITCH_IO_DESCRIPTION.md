## Game Title

Jump Droid

## Tagline

Ascend Beyond the Atmosphere

## Short Description

A precision vertical rocket exploration game built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose.

## Full Description

Pilot a droid-controlled rocket through 12 hostile atmospheric zones. Manage fuel, heat, shields, and hull integrity as you ascend from Earth's surface toward The Void and The Singularity beyond.

**What awaits you:**

- **12 atmospheric zones** — from Earth's blue skies to the reality-warped Singularity, each with unique parallax backgrounds and dynamic BGM
- **11 boss encounters** — multi-phase fights with weak point targeting, each with its own AI and attack patterns
- **26+ threat types** — environmental hazards, enemy drones, mini-bosses, and screen-filling bosses
- **Build your rocket** — 4 rocket classes, 17 unlockable modules. Your rocket is a Build, not a vehicle
- **12-track mission system** — challenges that evolve from Rookie to God tier
- **Power-ups & artifacts** — rare drops with set bonuses for deep customization
- **Secret missions & lore** — hidden signals, blueprints, and Codex entries for thorough explorers
- **Prestige & Eternal Mode** — permanent progression beyond 100km and infinite capped scaling

**The Singularity** — the final meta-boss encounter at 100,000 meters features HUD Pull and control glitch mechanics. Defeat it to unlock the Ascension Ceremony, Omega Modules, and Eternal Mode.

### Built with

Kotlin + Jetpack Compose Canvas. No game engine. Open source (MIT).
