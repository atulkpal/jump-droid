*   All 50 missions (43 public + 7 hidden) are functional.

*   **Hidden Signals**: Cryptic hints are functional, but the "revealed" state lacks a dedicated discovery animation.
*   **Balance**: Higher-tier altitude missions (10,000m+) may require tuning once new Biomes are added.
