# Jump Droid — Monetization Vision Document

**Date:** 2026-06-22
**Project:** Jump Droid
**Objective:** Establish ethical revenue systems without compromising core gameplay integrity.

---

## 1. Core Principles

### **NO Pay-to-Win**
*   Power cannot be purchased. 
*   All gameplay-affecting Modules and Rocket Classes must be earned through Missions, Discovery, or Boss Rewards.

### **NO Progress Gating**
*   No "Energy" timers or cooldowns that stop play.
*   No purchasable mission skips.
*   No paywalls for Biomes or Zones.

---

## 2. Revenue Pillars

### **Supportive Ads**
*   **Rewarded Continue**: Optional ad to resume a run with 50% health. Max once per run.
*   **Menu Banners**: Standard banner ads restricted to the **Title Screen** and **Archive**. Never during flight.
*   **Premium Purchase**: One-time $2.99 purchase to remove all non-rewarded ads permanently.

### **Cosmetic Economy**
*   **Rocket Skins**: High-detail chassis variants (e.g. Chrome, Stealth, Prototype-X).
*   **Engine Trails**: Custom particle colors and patterns (e.g. Plasma Blue, Solar Flare, Void Glitch).
*   **UI Themes**: Alternate HUD aesthetics for the main gauges and Mission Screen.
*   **Celebration Packs**: Custom VFX for Mission Completion and Rank Up ceremonies.

### **Expansions (Long-term)**
*   **Fleet Packs**: Bundles of 3 unique chassis with specific stat profiles (balanced for existing classes).
*   **The Outer Reaches**: Massive content updates adding new Biomes, Lore, and Missions.

---

## 3. Implementation Order
1.  **Phase 1**: Ad Framework & Removals (MVP).
2.  **Phase 2**: Chassis Skins (The Hangar).
3.  **Phase 3**: Particle Customization (Engine Trails).
4.  **Phase 4**: Expansion Content (Biomes).
