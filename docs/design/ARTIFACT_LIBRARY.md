# Jump Droid: Artifact Library

This document is the permanent content vault for all Artifacts and Discoveries in Jump Droid, including implemented records and future lore-bearing items.

---

## A. Existing Artifacts

These items are current discovery entries in the "ARTIFACTS" category.

### 1. Flight Recorder
*   **Description:** A damaged recording unit from a previous mission.
*   **Lore Value:** Contains garbled audio describing 'structures in the clouds'.
*   **Gameplay Value:** High category completion progress.
*   **Discovery Method:** Random drop from `PowerUpType.ARTIFACT`.

### 2. Unknown Alloy
*   **Description:** A fragment of crystalline material.
*   **Lore Value:** Its molecular structure suggests it was grown, not forged.
*   **Gameplay Value:** High category completion progress.
*   **Discovery Method:** Random drop from `PowerUpType.ARTIFACT`.

### 3. Encrypted Beacon
*   **Description:** A signaling device continuously broadcasting a single prime number.
*   **Lore Value:** Found drifting in orbit; source unknown.
*   **Gameplay Value:** High category completion progress.
*   **Discovery Method:** Random drop from `PowerUpType.ARTIFACT`.

### 4. Drone Core
*   **Description:** An automated survey unit from an early expedition.
*   **Lore Value:** AI seems stuck in a loop repeating 'The Void calls'.
*   **Gameplay Value:** High category completion progress.
*   **Discovery Method:** Random drop from `PowerUpType.ARTIFACT`.

## B. Future Artifacts

Proposed artifacts to deepen the game's mystery.

### 1. Pre-Signal Map
*   **Classification:** APPROVED
*   **Description:** A primitive star chart dating before the Ascension Program.
*   **Lore Value:** Shows a path through the Void that is no longer there.
*   **Gameplay Value:** Unlocks a "Lore View" in the Archive.
*   **Status:** APPROVED.

### 2. Biomechanical Shard
*   **Classification:** APPROVED
*   **Description:** A pulsing piece of metal and organic tissue.
*   **Lore Value:** Proof that some threats are more than machine.
*   **Gameplay Value:** Increases permanent damage reduction by 1% (Artifact Set bonus concept).
*   **Status:** APPROVED.

### 3. The Architect's Signature
*   **Classification:** APPROVED (EPIC 11)
*   **Description:** A cryptographic key containing the builder's identity.
*   **Lore Value:** The ultimate proof of the project's purpose.
*   **Gameplay Value:** Required for "Prestige" resets.
*   **Status:** APPROVED.

### 4. Frozen Signal
*   **Classification:** BACKLOG
*   **Description:** A data drive containing a physical manifestation of a transmission.
*   **Lore Value:** Hinting at the "Signal" being a sentient entity.
*   **Gameplay Value:** Large score bonus upon recovery.
*   **Status:** BACKLOG.

### 5. Golden Rocket Shell
*   **Classification:** REJECTED
*   **Rationale:** Purely cosmetic artifacts without lore or gameplay value are secondary to the "Vertical Mystery" design goal.
*   **Status:** REJECTED.
