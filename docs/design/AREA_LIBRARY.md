# Jump Droid: Area Library

This document is the permanent content vault for all atmospheric zones in Jump Droid, detailing their unique identities, hazards, and future expansions.

---

## A. Existing Areas

The current ascent journey spans twelve distinct altitude zones across four atmospheric tiers.

### 1. Earth (Ground Zero)
*   **Altitude:** 0m - 500m
*   **Theme:** The familiar home world.
*   **Visual Identity:** Blue-to-white gradients, distant mountains, and rolling hills in the background.
*   **Hazards:** None (Tutorial zone).
*   **Enemies:** Surveyor Probe (Scout).
*   **Bosses:** None.
*   **Discoveries:** Standard Platform, Fuel Tank.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Establishing basic flight controls and landing rhythm.

### 2. Cloud Layer
*   **Altitude:** 500m - 1500m
*   **Theme:** Stormy weather and high-altitude winds.
*   **Visual Identity:** Cyan-to-blue gradients, dense layers of moving white clouds.
*   **Hazards:** Lightning Storm, Turbulence Front.
*   **Enemies:** Sky Ray, Aerosol Swarm.
*   **Bosses:** None.
*   **Discoveries:** Ice Platform, Moving Platform.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Introduction of environmental forces and slippery physics.

### 3. Upper Atmosphere
*   **Altitude:** 1500m - 4000m
*   **Theme:** The thinning edge of the sky.
*   **Visual Identity:** Deep purple-to-black gradients, faint stars, and nebulae start to appear.
*   **Hazards:** Debris Field.
*   **Enemies:** Aerosol Swarm (Reinforced).
*   **Bosses:** None (Mini-Boss: Command Cruiser).
*   **Discoveries:** Breakable Platform, Phase Platform, Utility Platforms.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Resource management becomes critical; introduction of temporary landing spots.

### 4. Orbit
*   **Altitude:** 4000m - 8000m
*   **Theme:** Low Earth Orbit wreckage.
*   **Visual Identity:** Pitch black sky, high star density, Earth's curvature visible at the bottom.
*   **Hazards:** EMP Pulse, Solar Flare, Radiation Zone.
*   **Enemies:** Defense Node (Orbital Sentry).
*   **Bosses:** The Gatekeeper.
*   **Discoveries:** Artifact Beacon.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Avoiding radar scans and managing massive solar heat spikes.

### 5. Deep Space
*   **Altitude:** 8000m - 12000m
*   **Theme:** The uncharted silent sea.
*   **Visual Identity:** Absolute black background with pulsing blue/purple nebulae.
*   **Hazards:** Gravity Distortion, Mirror Shards, Gravity Shear.
*   **Enemies:** Void Tracker (Stalker - Implemented), Cosmic Leviathan (Whale - Implemented), Derelict Echo.
*   **Bosses:** The Leviathan, Star-Eater, Entropy Core.
*   **Discoveries:** Unknown Alloy, Flight Recorder.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Massive gravity-shifting entities and resource competition.

### 6. The Void
*   **Altitude:** 12000m - 16000m
*   **Theme:** Reality-warping anomaly zone.
*   **Visual Identity:** Pure Black, reality distortion ripples, distorted star patterns.
*   **Hazards:** Void Anomaly, Mirror Shards.
*   **Enemies:** Shadow Entity (Wraith - Implemented).
*   **Bosses:** Void Engine, The Signal.
*   **Discoveries:** Drone Core.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Survival at the limits of physics; deceptive platforms and HUD interference.

### 7. The Foundry
*   **Altitude:** 16000m - 20000m
*   **Theme:** Ancient automated manufacturing belt in high orbit.
*   **Visual Identity:** Mechanical structures, laser grids, sparks flying in the vacuum, metal scaffolding.
*   **Hazards:** EMP Pulse, Solar Flare.
*   **Enemies:** Automated Welders, Security Drones.
*   **Bosses:** The Architect.
*   **Discoveries:** Industrial Artifacts, Forger's Mark.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Fast-moving platforms and industrial hazards. Platform manipulation is the key mechanic.

### 8. Chrono-Rift
*   **Altitude:** 20000m - 24000m
*   **Theme:** Fractured region where time flows irregularly.
*   **Visual Identity:** Purple/cyan glitch effects, background objects at different frame rates, time-distortion ripples.
*   **Hazards:** Void Anomaly, Gravity Shear.
*   **Enemies:** Phase Wraith.
*   **Bosses:** None (future: Chrono Warden).
*   **Discoveries:** Lost Expedition Logs, Temporal Fragment.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Time-dilation bubbles and glitchy HUD interference. Overheat management is critical.

### 9. The Beyond
*   **Altitude:** 24000m - 30000m
*   **Theme:** Where matter blurs into pure energy.
*   **Visual Identity:** Shifting color gradients, particle nebulae, semi-transparent geometry.
*   **Hazards:** Cryo-Mist, Radiation Zone.
*   **Enemies:** Void Harvester, Heat Bat.
*   **Bosses:** None.
*   **Discoveries:** Ethereal Shard, Beyond Echo.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Extreme temperature duality — freezing mist and radiation zones coexist.

### 10. Stellar Gate
*   **Altitude:** 30000m - 40000m
*   **Theme:** The artificial sky — a construct older than civilization.
*   **Visual Identity:** Massive geometric gate structures, star-field warps, energy conduits.
*   **Hazards:** Gravity Distortion, EMP Pulse.
*   **Enemies:** Scout Drone (Reinforced), Orbital Sentry.
*   **Bosses:** None.
*   **Discoveries:** Gate Fragment, Ancient Codex.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Navigating through mega-structures with reinforced enemy encounters.

### 11. Ancient Construct
*   **Altitude:** 40000m - 80000m
*   **Theme:** The heart in the static — the origin of the signal.
*   **Visual Identity:** Organic-mechanical hybrid architecture, pulsing energy veins, void-light.
*   **Hazards:** Void Anomaly, Cryo-Mist.
*   **Enemies:** Phase Wraith, Void Harvester.
*   **Bosses:** None.
*   **Discoveries:** Construct Core, Origin Glyph.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** The final stretch before the singularity. Every system is pushed to the limit.

### 12. Singularity (Point Zero)
*   **Altitude:** 100,000m+
*   **Theme:** Non-Euclidean white-noise space. The end of the climb.
*   **Visual Identity:** Blinding white background, geometric artifacts, HUD glitches, gravity flux.
*   **Hazards:** Physics Flux (Gravity and Thrust fluctuations), Control Inversion.
*   **Bosses:** The Singularity.
*   **Discoveries:** The Final Truth, The Architect's Signature.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** The ultimate test of pilot rhythm and adaptation. Origin resets to 0 after transition.

## B. Future Areas

Proposed zones for vertical and narrative expansion.

### 1. Event Horizon
*   **Classification:** BACKLOG
*   **Theme:** The absolute final climb beyond the singularity.
*   **Visual Identity:** White-noise particles, UI is distorted/corrupted, gravity constant changes.
*   **Hazards:** Singularity Pulses.
*   **Enemies:** Formless Horrors.
*   **Bosses:** None (post-finale).
*   **Discoveries:** Final Truth.
*   **Complexity:** Extreme. End-of-game development goal.

### 2. Subterranean Vents
*   **Classification:** REJECTED
*   **Rationale:** "Pre-Earth" descent conflicts with the core "Vertical Ascent" model established for Jump Droid.
