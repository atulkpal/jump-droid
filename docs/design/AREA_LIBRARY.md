# Jump Droid: Area Library

This document is the permanent content vault for all atmospheric zones in Jump Droid, detailing their unique identities, hazards, and future expansions.

---

## A. Existing Areas

The current ascent journey spans six distinct altitude zones.

### 1. Earth (Ground Zero)
*   **Altitude:** 0m - 500m
*   **Theme:** The familiar home world.
*   **Visual Identity:** Blue-to-white gradients, distant mountains, and rolling hills in the background.
*   **Hazards:** None (Tutorial zone).
*   **Enemies:** Surveyor Probe (Scout).
*   **Bosses:** None.
*   **Discoveries:** Standard Platform, Fuel Tank.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Establishing basic flight controls and landing rhythm.

### 2. Cloud Layer
*   **Altitude:** 500m - 1500m
*   **Theme:** Stormy weather and high-altitude winds.
*   **Visual Identity:** Cyan-to-blue gradients, dense layers of moving white clouds.
*   **Hazards:** Lightning Storm, Turbulence Front.
*   **Enemies:** Sky Ray, Aerosol Swarm.
*   **Bosses:** None.
*   **Discoveries:** Ice Platform, Moving Platform.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Introduction of environmental forces and slippery physics.

### 3. Upper Atmosphere
*   **Altitude:** 1500m - 4000m
*   **Theme:** The thinning edge of the sky.
*   **Visual Identity:** Deep purple-to-black gradients, faint stars, and nebulae start to appear.
*   **Hazards:** Debris Field.
*   **Enemies:** Aerosol Swarm (Reinforced).
*   **Bosses:** None (Mini-Boss: Command Cruiser).
*   **Discoveries:** Breakable Platform, Phase Platform, Utility Platforms.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Resource management becomes critical; introduction of temporary landing spots.

### 4. Orbit
*   **Altitude:** 4000m - 8000m
*   **Theme:** Low Earth Orbit wreckage.
*   **Visual Identity:** Pitch black sky, high star density, Earth's curvature visible at the bottom.
*   **Hazards:** EMP Pulse, Solar Flare, Radiation Zone.
*   **Enemies:** Defense Node (Orbital Sentry).
*   **Bosses:** The Gatekeeper.
*   **Discoveries:** Artifact Beacon.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Avoiding radar scans and managing massive solar heat spikes.

### 5. Deep Space
*   **Altitude:** 8000m - 15000m
*   **Theme:** The uncharted silent sea.
*   **Visual Identity:** Absolute black background with pulsing blue/purple nebulae.
*   **Hazards:** Gravity Distortion.
*   **Enemies:** Void Tracker (Stalker - Implemented), Cosmic Leviathan (Whale - Implemented), Derelict Echo.
*   **Bosses:** The Leviathan, Star-Eater.
*   **Discoveries:** Unknown Alloy, Flight Recorder.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Massive gravity-shifting entities and resource competition.

### 6. The Void
*   **Altitude:** 15000m+
*   **Theme:** Reality-warping anomaly zone.
*   **Visual Identity:** Pure Black, reality distortion ripples, distorted star patterns.
*   **Hazards:** Void Anomaly.
*   **Enemies:** Shadow Entity (Wraith - Implemented).
*   **Bosses:** Void Engine, The Signal.
*   **Discoveries:** Drone Core.
*   **Gameplay Identity:** Survival at the limits of physics; deceptive platforms and HUD interference.

## B. Future Areas

Proposed zones for vertical and narrative expansion.

### 1. The Foundry
*   **Classification:** APPROVED
*   **Theme:** An ancient, automated manufacturing belt in high orbit.
*   **Visual Identity:** Mechanical structures, laser grids, and sparks flying in the vacuum.
*   **Hazards:** Moving Laser Grids, Crusher Blocks.
*   **Enemies:** Automated Welders, Security Drones.
*   **Bosses:** The Architect.
*   **Discoveries:** Industrial Artifacts.
*   **Complexity:** High. Requires significant background asset sets.

### 2. Chrono-Rift
*   **Classification:** APPROVED
*   **Theme:** A fractured region of space where time flows irregularly.
*   **Visual Identity:** Purple/Cyan glitch effects, background objects moving at different frame rates.
*   **Hazards:** Time-Dilation Bubbles.
*   **Enemies:** Ghost Echoes.
*   **Bosses:** Chrono Warden.
*   **Discoveries:** Lost Expedition Logs.
*   **Complexity:** Very High. Requires specialized shaders and time-warp logic.

### 3. Event Horizon
*   **Classification:** BACKLOG
*   **Theme:** The absolute final climb before the ultimate mystery.
*   **Visual Identity:** White-noise particles, UI is distorted/corrupted, gravity constant changes.
*   **Hazards:** Singularity Pulses.
*   **Enemies:** Formless Horrors.
*   **Bosses:** The Singularity.
*   **Discoveries:** Final Truth.
*   **Complexity:** Extreme. End-of-game development goal.

### 4. Subterranean Vents
*   **Classification:** REJECTED
*   **Rationale:** "Pre-Earth" descent conflicts with the core "Vertical Ascent" model established for Jump Droid.
*   **Status:** REJECTED.
