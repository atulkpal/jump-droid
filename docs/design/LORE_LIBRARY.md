# Jump Droid: Lore Library

This document is the permanent content vault for the narrative and history of Jump Droid.

---

## A. Existing Lore

Currently documented lore fragments within the "LORE" category.

### 1. The Ascension Program
*   **Summary:** Multinational effort to reach the source of the 'Great Signal'.
*   **Discovery Method:** Automated upon first reaching Orbit.
*   **Related Area:** Orbit.

### 2. The First Signal
*   **Summary:** Originated from the Void; sequence matched no known language.
*   **Discovery Method:** Automated upon first reaching Cloud Layer.
*   **Related Area:** Void.

### 3. The Lost Fleet
*   **Summary:** Records of multiple vessels that vanished during the initial push.
*   **Discovery Method:** Found after discovering enough Artifacts.
*   **Related Area:** Deep Space.

### 4. The Ascension Logs
*   **Summary:** Encrypted logs suggesting the signal might be internal to the planet.
*   **Discovery Method:** Achievement-based lore unlock.
*   **Related Area:** Earth.
